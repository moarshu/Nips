Our Story

NIPS College of IT & Management was established with a vision to provide quality, affordable, and industry-relevant education in Information Technology and Management. Over the years, we have successfully trained thousands of students who are now contributing significantly to the IT industry across India and globally.

Our college is committed to fostering an environment where students can develop both technical and soft skills, preparing them for the competitive job market while maintaining the highest standards of academic excellence.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education in Information Technology and Management, empowering students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to contribute positively to society while building successful careers.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in IT and Management education, recognized for academic excellence, industry collaboration, and producing globally competitive professionals who drive innovation and growth.
