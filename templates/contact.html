Frequently Asked Questions

When do admissions start?

Admissions are open throughout the year. You can apply anytime based on your convenience and the course availability. Contact our admission team for specific intake dates.

What is the fee structure?

Our fee structure is competitive and reasonable. Fees vary based on the course you choose. We offer flexible payment options and scholarships for deserving candidates.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, our dedicated placement cell provides comprehensive job placement assistance. We have partnerships with leading IT companies across India.

Can I pursue part-time courses?

Yes, we offer both full-time and part-time options for most of our courses. This flexibility allows working professionals to upskill without leaving their jobs.

What about hostel facilities?

We have hostel facilities available for outstation students. Separate hostels for boys and girls with all modern amenities are provided.

How can I get a scholarship?

We offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Contact our admissions office for eligibility criteria and application process.
